# Update: Validator Election Issue

## What's happening?
After Referendum 1909, validators below the new minimum stake threshold became eligible to be permissionlessly chilled. Once replaced, the new validator set no longer met the minimum on-chain election score needed to complete validator rotation. A fix is underway.

## Impact
The current active era is extended until the fix is deployed. The validator set won't rotate and new nominations won't take effect until then. Aside from staking operations tied to era transitions, the network is operating normally.

## Rewards: delayed, not lost
Validators and nominators keep earning throughout the extended era. Because the era is longer than usual, the final payout will be proportionally larger. Only the timing of the payout changes, not the amount.

## When will it be fixed?
A referendum to update the on-chain minimum score has been posted. We estimate validator elections will resume within **2.5–4 days**. A full postmortem will follow once resolved.

Thank you for your patience.
